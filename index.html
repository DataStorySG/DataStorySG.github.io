About US

We have been collaborating with community partners since 2019 to make learning about data and AI tools more accessible.

This is done through our pro-bono, hands-on, guided programs that use storytelling as a medium.

The Team: We are a team of working professionals with a passion for technology. Our goal is to mitigate the risks of technology-induced disparities as much as we can.

Our impact to date

  • Taught over 600 students to date
  • More than 95% of students who had gone through our course would recommend it to their batchmates!
  • Collaborated with secondary schools and Junior colleges in Singapore (e.g. ACS)
  • Supported by National Youth Council and Majurity Trust

Our Programmes

1. Introduction to Data Science

This programme can range from a standalone afternoon session to a term-long programme depending on the preference of our partners. It aims to help students familiarise themselves with the data science workflow starting with basic statistical concepts before moving into other aspects such as cleaning, modelling and visualisation. These sessions are complemented with hands-on coding sessions with our annotated code books. Our goal is to help our students be able to deliver a story based on a problem space they are interested in based on the data that we have worked with them to discover. Some articles include deep dives of rent in Singapore, flight delays as well as housing.

View Sample Curriculum

2. Data Science Practicum

For partners who wish for a longer-term programme, we also offer a practicum phase for participants to revisit concepts that are covered in the Introduction phase and expose participants to more technical skills and storyboarding techniques. This phase is intended to be more code-focused. Participants are also expected to deliver a storytelling product.

View Sample Curriculum

3. Build with AI

Conceptualised as a term-long programme, the Build with AI course is designed to help participants understand when they can turn to AI to solve problems productively. In the long-term, the goal is to equip them with knowledge to be able to use tech to solve more problems.
